## Buying Guide

### GPU Performance

The GPU is the most critical component for gaming. We recommend at least an RTX 4060 for modern AAA games at high settings.

### Display Quality

Look for:
- 144Hz+ refresh rate
- 1440p resolution minimum
- 100% sRGB color gamut
- Low response time (3ms or less)

### Cooling System

Gaming laptops generate significant heat. Quality cooling systems ensure sustained performance and longevity.

## Conclusion

Gaming laptops in 2025 offer incredible performance without compromise. Choose based on your budget and specific gaming needs.
