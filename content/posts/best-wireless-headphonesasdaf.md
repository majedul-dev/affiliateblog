## What to Consider

When choosing wireless headphones, consider these factors:

1. **Sound Quality** - Look for balanced frequency response
2. **Battery Life** - Aim for 20+ hours minimum
3. **Comfort** - Essential for extended listening sessions
4. **Noise Cancellation** - Important for travel and commuting
5. **Price** - Determine your budget beforehand

Whether you prioritize sound quality, battery life, or budget, there's a perfect pair of wireless headphones for you in 2025. Our top recommendation is the Sony WH-1000XM5 for most users, but the Soundcore Q45 offers exceptional value for budget-conscious buyers.
